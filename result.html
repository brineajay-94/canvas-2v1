FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about NEB results and grade sheets at Canvas+2 College.

How can I check my result?
Select your result sheet from the dropdown, enter your symbol number, and click "Search Result". Your grade sheet with subject-wise grades and GPA will be displayed instantly.
What is the grading system for +2 in Nepal?
NEB uses a letter grading system from A+ (Outstanding) to NG (Non Graded). Each grade has a corresponding grade point from 4.0 down to 0.0. The minimum passing grade is D (35% or 1.6 GPA).
Can I download my grade sheet?
Yes, once your result is displayed, you can use the print option to save it as a PDF or take a screenshot for your records.
I cannot find my result. What should I do?
Make sure you have selected the correct result sheet and entered the exact symbol number. If the issue persists, contact the college administration office at Triyuga-11, Gaighat, Udayapur, or call 9851321240.
Are results for both Class 11 and Class 12 available?
Yes, we publish results for both Class 11 and Class 12. Simply select the appropriate result sheet from the dropdown based on your class and program.
