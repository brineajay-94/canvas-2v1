FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is GPA calculated in NEB?
GPA is calculated by dividing the total grade points earned by the total credit hours. For each subject, the grade point (e.g., A+ = 4.0) is multiplied by the credit hours to get the grade points earned. Sum all grade points and divide by total credit hours to get your GPA.
What credit hours do NEB subjects have?
NEB assigns credit hours to each subject based on the curriculum. Common subjects like English, Nepali, and Mathematics typically have 4-5 credit hours, while elective subjects may have 2-3 credit hours. Check your NEB curriculum for exact credit hours per subject.
What is a good GPA in NEB Class 11 and 12?
In the NEB system, a GPA of 3.6 (A) or above is considered excellent. A GPA of 3.2 (B+) is very good, and 2.8 (B) is good. Most competitive university programs expect at least a 2.8-3.2 GPA for admission.
Can I use this calculator for both Class 11 and 12?
Yes! This GPA calculator works for both NEB Class 11 and Class 12 results. Simply add all your subjects with their respective credit hours and grades. The GPA is calculated using the standard NEB 4.0 grading scale.
What does NG (Non Graded) mean?
NG stands for Non Graded. It is assigned when a student does not meet the minimum requirements for a grade in a subject. NG carries 0 grade points and does not contribute to your GPA calculation.
How to use this NEB GPA Calculator for Class 11?
To use this NEB GPA Calculator for Class 11, add all your subjects from the Class 11 NEB curriculum with their correct credit hours and select the grade you obtained for each subject. The calculator will automatically compute your GPA using the NEB 4.0 grading scale.
How to calculate Class 12 GPA using this calculator?
Calculating your Class 12 GPA is easy with this online GPA Calculator Nepal tool. Simply enter each subject from your Class 12 NEB curriculum, input the credit hours, and select your grades. The GPA is computed instantly using the standard NEB formula: total grade points divided by total credit hours.
What is the highest GPA possible in NEB?
The highest possible GPA in the NEB system is 4.0, which requires achieving A+ (90% or above) in all subjects. This NEB GPA calculator uses the same 4.0 scale to compute your grade point average accurately.
