Campus Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities at Canvas+2

We provide a comprehensive range of facilities to ensure a holistic educational experience for every student.

Library

Our well-stocked library houses thousands of reference books, textbooks, journals, and digital resources across management, science, and humanities. Students have access to a quiet reading hall and borrowing services throughout the academic year.

Computer Lab

Fully equipped computer lab with high-speed internet and modern systems running the latest educational software. Students gain hands-on experience with productivity tools, programming environments, and digital learning platforms essential for modern education.

Science Lab

State-of-the-art science laboratory for physics, chemistry, and biology practical sessions. The lab is equipped with modern instruments, safety equipment, and specimens that allow students to perform experiments that reinforce theoretical concepts.

Sports Ground

Spacious sports ground and indoor game facilities including volleyball, basketball, badminton, table tennis, and athletics. Regular sports events and inter-college competitions help students stay active and develop teamwork skills.

Cafeteria

Hygienic and spacious cafeteria serving nutritious meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices. The cafeteria maintains high standards of cleanliness and provides a comfortable space for students to relax and socialize during breaks.

Smart Classrooms

All classrooms are equipped with multimedia projectors, sound systems, and modern teaching aids. Our smart classrooms create an interactive learning environment where teachers can use presentations, videos, and digital content to enhance understanding.

Transportation

Reliable bus service covering major routes across Triyuga, Gaighat, and surrounding areas. Our transportation fleet ensures safe and punctual commutes for students coming from different parts of Udayapur district and nearby regions.

Hostel

Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with comfortable accommodation, 24-hour security, study rooms, and recreational spaces. The hostels provide a home-like environment with dedicated wardens ensuring student well-being and discipline.
