FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about admission at Canvas +2 College of Science and Management.

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

Students who have successfully completed SEE (Secondary Education Examination) from any recognized board are eligible to apply for the Plus Two program. For BBA and BBS programs, applicants must have completed their +2 or equivalent with a minimum of D+ in all subjects.

What documents are required for admission?

You will need to submit: original SEE/+2 mark sheet and character certificate, recent passport-size photographs (4 copies), citizenship certificate or birth certificate, transfer certificate from the previous institution, and any other relevant academic documents.

When does the admission process start and end?

Admissions for the new academic session typically begin right after the SEE and +2 board results are published. The process continues until all seats are filled. We encourage early applications as seats are limited. Contact our administration office for the exact dates.

Does the college offer scholarships?

Yes, Canvas +2 College of Science and Management offers merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students. Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance in previous examinations, entrance test results, and financial need. Please contact our office for detailed scholarship information.
