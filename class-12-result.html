About NEB Class 12 Result

The National Examination Board (NEB) of Nepal conducts the Grade 12 examination for students across the country. Results are published online on the official NEB portal and are also accessible through affiliated colleges like Canvas+2 College of Management. Students can check their results using their unique symbol number. The NEB grading system uses a letter-based GPA scale from A+ to E, reflecting students' performance across all subjects. Canvas+2 College ensures timely result publication and provides guidance for result analysis and further academic pathways.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about NEB Class 12 results answered.

How can I check my NEB Class 12 result?
You can check your NEB Class 12 result by entering your 9-digit symbol number in the search box above. Results are also available on the official NEB website at neb.gov.np.
When are NEB Grade 12 results published?
NEB typically publishes Grade 12 results within 3-4 months after the completion of the final examination. Results for the academic year 2080/2081 are expected around Jestha-Ashad (May-June).
What is the passing grade for NEB Class 12?
The minimum passing grade is D (1.6 GPA). Students must achieve at least a D grade in each subject to pass the NEB Class 12 examination.
Can I get a physical copy of my grade sheet?
Yes, physical grade sheets are distributed by NEB through your respective college. Canvas+2 College will notify students when physical certificates are available for collection.
How is the NEB GPA calculated?
NEB GPA is calculated by dividing the total grade points earned across all subjects by the total credit hours. Each grade point is multiplied by the subject's credit hour, summed, and divided by total credit hours.
