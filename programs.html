Our Academic Programs

Discover your path to success with our diverse range of programs

Program

Plus Two (Management)

A comprehensive management program for classes 11 and 12 under the National Examination Board (NEB), designed to build a strong foundation in business and commerce.

Duration

Two years (Class 11 & 12) with yearly examination system as per NEB curriculum.

Core Subjects

  • English
  • Nepali
  • Accountancy
  • Mathematics or Social Studies
  • Business Mathematics
  • Hotel Management / Computer Science

Career Options

  • BBA / BBS / BBM
  • CA / ACCA / CFA
  • Banking & Finance
  • Civil Service
  • Hotel & Tourism Management
  • Entrepreneurship

Why This Program

Our Management program combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills through projects, presentations, and real-world case studies, preparing students for both higher education and professional careers.

Program

Plus Two (Science)

A rigorous science program for classes 11 and 12 under NEB, preparing students for medical, engineering, and pure science careers with state-of-the-art lab facilities.

Duration

Two years (Class 11 & 12) with practical examinations and theory papers as per NEB science curriculum.

Core Subjects

  • English
  • Nepali
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology or Computer Science
  • Mathematics

Career Options

  • MBBS / BDS / Nursing
  • Engineering (BE / B.Tech)
  • Pharmacy / Biotechnology
  • Computer Science & IT
  • Research & Academia
  • Civil Service

Why This Program

Well-equipped science laboratories, experienced faculty, and a balanced focus on theory and practical work ensure students are well-prepared for competitive entrance exams and higher studies.

Bachelor's Program

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Affiliated with Rajarshi Janaki University (RJU), our BBA program offers a modern, practical approach to business education with a focus on leadership and innovation.

Duration

Four years (8 semesters) with project work, internships, and research components integrated into the curriculum.

Key Areas

  • Marketing Management
  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Business Law & Ethics
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Project-based Learning

Career Options

  • Business Analyst
  • Marketing Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • HR Specialist
  • Management Consultant
  • Entrepreneur / Startup Founder

Why This Program

Industry-aligned curriculum, case-study methodology, guest lectures from industry experts, and internship opportunities ensure graduates are job-ready from day one.

Bachelor's Program

Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU), our BBS program provides a strong theoretical foundation in business and commerce with a comprehensive examination-focused approach.

Duration

Four years (8 semesters) with annual examinations and a compulsory research project in the final year.

Key Areas

  • Economics
  • Auditing & Assurance
  • Taxation
  • Business Statistics
  • Accounting & Finance
  • Business Environment

Career Options

  • Accountant / Auditor
  • Banking Professional
  • Tax Consultant
  • Financial Analyst
  • Government Service
  • Higher Education (MBA / MBS)

Why This Program

TU affiliation ensures nationally recognized qualifications, experienced faculty, and a structured curriculum that prepares students for professional certifications and government examinations.
