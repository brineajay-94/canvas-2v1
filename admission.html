FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the admission process at Canvas +2 College of Science, Law and Management.

When does the admission start for the academic session?
Admission for the new academic session typically starts right after the SEE and +2 results are published. The exact dates are announced on the college notice board and website. For the current session , admission is now open.
What is the fee structure for different programs?
The fee structure varies by program. Please visit the college administration office for a detailed fee breakdown. We offer installment payment options and various scholarships to make education accessible.
Is there an entrance examination for admission?
For Plus Two programs, admission is primarily based on SEE scores. For BBA, candidates must appear for the CMAT entrance exam or the college-level entrance test. BBS admission is based on +2 merit.
Can I apply for multiple programs at the same time?
Yes, you can apply for multiple programs using separate application forms. However, you will need to confirm your seat in only one program if selected in more than one.
Does the college provide hostel or transportation facilities?
The college assists students in finding accommodation near the campus. Transportation facilities are available for students commuting from nearby areas. Contact the administration for details.
What scholarships are available for new students?
We offer merit-based, need-based, girls', and community scholarships. Merit scholarships are awarded based on SEE or +2 performance, while need-based scholarships require a financial assessment. Details are available at the office.
