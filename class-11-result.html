About NEB Class 11

NEB Grade 11 Result Overview

The National Examinations Board (NEB) conducts the Class 11 (Grade 11) examinations annually for students across Nepal. At Canvas +2 College of Science and Management, we offer both Management and Science streams under the NEB curriculum. Results are typically published within 6–8 weeks after the final examinations, and students can check their results online using their symbol number.

Exam Schedule

Class 11 exams are held annually in Baisakh-Jestha (April-May) with supplementary exams in Shrawan-Bhadra (July-August).

Grading System

NEB follows a letter grading system from A+ to NG based on the GPA obtained in each subject and overall performance.

Result Publication

Results are published on the official NEB website and mirrored on college portals for easy access by students.

Re-totaling & Re-checking

Students can apply for re-totaling or re-checking of answer sheets within a stipulated time after result publication.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about NEB Class 11 results and the checking process.

When are NEB Class 11 results published?
NEB Class 11 results are typically published within 6–8 weeks after the final examinations. For the annual exams held in Baisakh-Jestha (April-May), results usually come out in Ashadh-Shrawan (June-July). Supplementary exam results follow a similar timeline.
How can I check my Class 11 result online?
You can check your result by entering your NEB symbol number in the search form above. Results are also available on the official NEB website at neb.gov.np and through SMS services provided by NEB.
What is the grading system for NEB Class 11?
NEB uses a letter grading system with grades ranging from A+ (4.0 GPA, 90–100%) to NG (Not Graded). The overall GPA is calculated based on the grade points obtained in all subjects. Refer to the GPA conversion table above for detailed information.
What should I do if my symbol number is not found?
If your symbol number is not found, please double-check the number on your admit card. If the issue persists, contact the college administration or your class teacher. Results may take a few hours to sync across all platforms after official publication.
Can I apply for re-totaling or re-checking?
Yes, NEB allows students to apply for re-totaling or re-checking of answer sheets within a specified period after the result publication. You can submit your application through the college administration. A nominal fee is charged per subject.
What is the minimum GPA required to pass Class 11?
To pass NEB Class 11, a student must obtain a minimum GPA of 1.6 (D+) in each subject and an overall GPA of at least 1.6. A student receiving NG (Not Graded) in any subject will not be considered passed.
